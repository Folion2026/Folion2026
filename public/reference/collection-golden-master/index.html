Fennell Bay Master Plan

Fennell Bay, NSW 2021 Landcom
Project Narrative

Fennell Bay Master Plan establishes a framework for a new residential community embedded within the site's bushland, ridgeline and creek systems. The proposal uses biodiversity, topography and water as structuring elements, organising the community around Marmong Creek, retained woodland, blue-green corridors and three walkable micro-neighbourhoods. A Community Hub and park form the social heart of the plan, supported by housing diversity, active transport and stronger connections to the wider Lake Macquarie community.
